About Us

At Gems Bag, we believe in the beauty of individuality. Each of our handmade beaded bags tells a story, crafted with love and attention to detail. Our mission is to provide you with unique accessories that reflect your personal style.

LAIBA BASRA

Founder & Designer

With a passion for craftsmanship, Laiba founded Gems Bag to bring unique, handmade accessories to life.

Our Materials

We take pride in sourcing the highest quality beads and materials from trusted suppliers both locally and internationally to ensure the best quality for our bags.

  • Sources: Local markets in Karachi, specialty bead shops in Istanbul, and sustainable suppliers from India.
  • Material Quality: Eco-friendly, handpicked beads and fabrics made to last.

Delivery Process

We ensure each bag is carefully packed and shipped to you using reliable courier services, providing tracking and timely delivery.

  • Packaging: Sustainable and protective packaging materials.
  • Shipping: Delivered via reliable couriers including FedEx, DHL, and local postal services.
  • Tracking: You will receive a tracking number to monitor your order progress.
